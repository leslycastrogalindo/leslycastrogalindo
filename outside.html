Favorite William & Mary Memory

One of my favorite memories at William & Mary has been spending time at Sunken Gardens with my friends. I have had countless picnics and club meetings, and even watched solar eclipses there. In my second year, I lived at Chandler Hall, which is less than a minute's walk away. No matter what, Sunken Garden holds endless memories, and I couldn't ask for anything more.

Favorite Trip

One of my favorite trips was summer 2024, when my younger sister and I flew to Los Angeles, California. While we were there, we explored Hollywood, Malibu, Santa Monica, Disneyland, drove up to Sequoia, and visited Yosemite. It was a busy week, but it was one of my favorite trips ever!
