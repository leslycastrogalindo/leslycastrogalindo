Favorite William & Mary Memory

One of my favorite memories at William & Mary has been spending time with my club soccer team. Traveling for games, team dinners, and late night laughs created friendships that made my college experience incredibly meaningful. Being part of a community that pushed each other both on and off the field is something I will always value.

Favorite Trip

One of my favorite trips was visiting a new city with friends and exploring different restaurants, walking through local neighborhoods, and learning about the culture. Traveling gives me a chance to step away from the routine of school and experience new places and perspectives.
